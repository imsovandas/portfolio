About Me

Hi, I'm Sovan Das – someone who's genuinely passionate about cybersecurity and everything that comes with it. I've done my Bachelor's in Digital Forensics, and over time, I've built hands-on experience through internships, labs, Capture the Flag (CTF) challenges, and real-world projects. Right now, I'm interning as a Penetration Tester at TCR Security, focusing on offensive security and learning new things every day.

I enjoy getting into the nitty-gritty of how systems function — and how they can be broken. Whether it's tracing digital clues, solving technical puzzles, or understanding cyber threats, I love the process of investigation and problem-solving.

I also believe knowledge grows when it's shared. I regularly post insights on LinkedIn, contribute tools and write-ups on GitHub, and run a YouTube channel called Das InfoSec, where I share tutorials and practical tips on cybersecurity.

At this stage, I'm actively looking for an entry-level cybersecurity role where I can grow, contribute meaningfully, and be part of something impactful. If you're into Cybersecurity, Cybercrime, AI, or Tech in general — feel free to connect! I'm always up for a good conversation and learning something new.
